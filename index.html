Industrial Automation team consists of two Field Engineers, Lingesh and Manikanta. They have combined experience of 6 years working on various control panels, different varieties of PLCs and CNCs.
Operational Technology
Operational Technology team consists of four electrical communication engineers, Vivek, Harsha, Mahendra, and Satish. Information Technology
Information Technology team consists of three computer science engineers, Vignan, Ashok and Harika. The team works with the latest technologies such as Flutter for Mobile, Go and Rust Programming Languages to produce desired results. The team is proficient with NodeJS and MongoDB.
Our Methodology is crafted in a way to suffice our client’s unique requirements. We believe that every problem needs to be understood from all perspectives, thus, we embrace a comprehensive approach.
Getting to know first
Our team first learns everything about you and your needs and specifications and current processes by adopting a user-focused approach.
Exploring the industry
We believe that we can find the best solution for a problem with extensive analysis. Exploring the industry and learning through observation, interviews with the clients, interacting with the domain experts, workshops, surveys and research helps us to obtain more insights into the problem and the scenario.
Customizing the service
After fully understanding the gravity of the system, our team works with the best-suited technologies to solve the problem most efficiently and economically possible.
Instigating the solutions
Our team will now propose a variety of suitable solutions to the problem after concentrated research and validate which solution best fits in the TQM(Time, Quality, Money) triangle.
Leaping into action
With the best understanding of the solution framework, we’ll now move into action and provide a detailed road map with all the specifications listed down to implement the designed solution.
Developing, integrating & testing
The detailed plan comes into play now after we have everything in place. We move into the execution phase by using a methodology(Waterfall/Agile/Lean) which best fits the technology stack that we chose and meticulously perform the quality assurance testing.
The end is never the end. It’s just a beginning.
We’ll continue to provide our ongoing support and maintenance to your needs with our faculty and develop a healthy long-term relationship with you.
Texoham

Soham Academy
Soham Academy of Human Excellence, established by Komaragiri Sahadev in 2016 to reform Indian Education System. This non-profit organization has been working with several schools to help students learn Yoga, Slokas, Robotics, and so many other things.
iFocus Mission
iFocus Mission focuses on bringing out the inherent positive qualities, promotes the culture of enriching one’s wisdom, aspires to help people effectively manage today’s world of complexities.
